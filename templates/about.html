About Dhrub Foundation

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Dhrub Foundation began as a small initiative to provide education to children in rural West Bengal. Today, we operate in 5 states, impacting over 50,000 lives annually.

Our name "Dhrub" comes from the Sanskrit word meaning "steadfast" - reflecting our commitment to sustainable change.

Mission

To empower marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and livelihood programs that create lasting change.

Vision

A world where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities to thrive.
